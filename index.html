Batman

In the name of his murdered parents, Bruce Wayne wages eternal war on the criminals of Gotham City. He is vengeance. He is the night. He is Batman.

Superman

Faster than a speeding bullet, more powerful than a locomotive… The Man of Steel fights a never-ending battle for truth, justice, and the American way.

The Flash

The Fastest Man Alive.The Flash has mastery over not just speed, but time itself, and he has often used his powers to travel though different eras and even into other dimensions. And he always does his best to prevent the same from happening to the people of Central City and Keystone City.

Cyborg

Part man, part machine, Vic Stone is a former member of the Teen Titans and a current member of the Justice League who wrestles to preserve his humanity with every new upgrade.

Aquaman

The son of a lighthouse keeper and an Atlantean queen, Arthur Curry is the bridge between the surface world and his tumultuous realm of the sea. Aquaman’s super-strength and fighting prowess alone make him a force to be reckoned with.
